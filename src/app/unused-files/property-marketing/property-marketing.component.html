Description:

We are delighted to offer to the market this wonderful light and bright, semi-detached family home located on this quiet private road in the heart of West Putney. The house benefits from off-street parking, an integral garage and a fantastic secluded south facing garden which also features a useful bicycle shed. This Scandinavian style house offers fantastic, flexible living and entertaining space and comprises a spectacular vaulted double height reception room with French doors to the garden and terrace, a spacious dining room, a fabulous double height conservatory which has the benefit of underfloor heating, a fully fitted eat-in kitchen and a W.C Upstairs, the principal bedroom is to the rear of the house with an ensuite bathroom with a bath and separate shower. Being at the back of the house, this affords excellent views of the garden. There are two other double bedrooms which are served by a family bathroom. To the front of the house, there is ample off-street parking for 3-4 cars and access to a good sized integral garage/utility room which could also be used as an office. The cost of a gardener is included in the rent. The house is located moments from Putney Common and is within easy access of Putney High Street, the River Thames and the amenities of Lower Richmond Road.
